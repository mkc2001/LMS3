All About the League

The Pokémon Battle League is commited to providing fun, organization, and competition to Pokémon players of all skill levels. Whether you're a hardcore expert or a beginner, you're bound to find a place here to learn and enjoy yourself. Our tournaments and meetups feature many different ways to play Pokémon.

Featured Pokémon:

Chikorita

Along with Cyndaquil and Totodile, Chikorita is one of the three starter Pokémon of Johto available at the beginning of Pokémon Gold, Silver, Crystal, HeartGold, and SoulSilver.On top of Chikorita's head is a large, green leaf that is usually longer than its body. A soothing aroma wafts from the leaf, which can calm battling Pokémon. Chikorita actively seeks out and enjoys the sun's warmth. Chikorita has a docile nature.
